about me ~

Hello! I’m Kaitlyn, a sophomore based in Northern California. This is my 4th year at SOHS as a full-time student, and I’m running for Board Chair of Student Academic Support and services (SASS). My main interests revolve around math and programming, and I love anything related to art or design! I also love designing + coding websites, as you can probably tell from this one :D.

Over the past few years, I’ve taken my fair share of OHS courses. I’ve also peer tutored quite a few of them (currently peer tutoring MSB, AP Chemistry, AP Calculus BC, and AP Computer Science A :D). In addition, I’ve been honored to work in SG before, where I created the first OHS Course Tips Event :), if you’ve heard of them. I hope to use the experience I’ve gained, coupled with the plans below, to help lighten your stress during the school year ;)

plans

Part 1: continue/improve current SASS resources


I plan on continuing the current SASS blog, which includes articles about OHS courses as well as interviews of OHS instructors, peer tutors, and TAs


I’d also love to host more weekly study meetings (current plan is to have ≈ 2-3 consistently per week, and we can poll for the best time :D) where we can just chill and study together :).

Part 2: expand


In addition to these current resources, I plan to expand the SASS website to include “information hubs” where we can share standardized testing (SAT/ACT/AP) sites, places to take classes at outside of OHS, transferring credit, etc. I spent way too much of my time researching information of the sort, so I hope that I’ll be able to at least partially relieve you of the same problems.
